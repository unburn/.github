# We're now [Unbrn](https://github.com/unbrn) 🔥

Hey everyone,

We have some exciting news to share — **Unburn is now Unbrn**.

## Why the change?

As we've grown, we wanted a name and identity that better reflects where we're headed. Unbrn keeps the spirit of what we started with, but with a sharper, more modern look that matches our pace as a tech company.

## What's changing

- **Name:** Unburn → Unbrn
- **Logo & visual identity:** Refreshed branding across our site, app, and socials
- **Brand colors:** New look and feel across all touchpoints
- **Slogan:** *"Simpler, by design"*

## What's NOT changing

- The product you know and use every day works the same way
- Our team, our mission, and our commitment to you stay unchanged

## What you need to do

Nothing! This is a rebrand, not a product change. You don't need to update anything on your end — we've handled the transition behind the scenes.

## Questions?

If you have any questions about the rebrand, feel free to reach out to our support team at **support@unbrn.com** (or wherever your support channel lives) — we're happy to help.

Thanks for being part of this journey with us.

— The Unbrn Team
